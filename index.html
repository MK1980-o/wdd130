Harare

Harare is the capital and largest city of Zimbabwe, located in the northeastern part of the country. It is the administrative, economic, and cultural hub of Zimbabwe, known for its vibrant arts scene, bustling markets, and beautiful parks such as the Harare Gardens. The city is also home to important institution the University of Zimbabwe. Harare's rich history, diverse culture, and warm climate make it a fascinating destination for visitors.
